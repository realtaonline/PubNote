A conforming processor is tolerant of foreign namespaces in XSL-FO XML instances. FOP is not tolerant, and so requires all non-XSL-FO constructs from the instance to be removed. This scrubs the PubNote constructs.

1.1. Straightforward exclusion from identity model

Scrub undesirables and keep everything else.

This is better than keeping only XSL-FO because sometimes XSL-FO has MathML, SVG, or other vocabularies recognized by a conforming processor.

match="pn:* | @pn:*" mode="#all" (xsl:template)

The identity template is used to copy all nodes not already being handled by other template rules.
